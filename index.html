Stage 3 is complete!

Your marbles just finished a quick and dirty Stage 3. I would like to remind you that this stage had a disadvantage for marble weight. The quick turns and numerous obstacles of this stage made lighter marble seem to perform better. But again, gravity is a formidable adversary.

After a fairly mediocre performance on the first two stages, Built-like-a-plank Barbs Rogers walked away with the number 1 spot for stage 3. She would like to thank her sponsors: The Bird/Hillmans, Artemis: The moon goddess, and Tate's Cookies. In a post race interview, Barbs “invoked the fifth amendment” 47 times.

Rory Walsh finished second for the day. He had a particularly heated match up with Barbs and finished only .289 seconds behind. She attributes today's performance to her beanstalk like build and her team's data-driven approach. The matchup has #PlankvsBeanstalk trending on Twitter.

Daniel Stewart finished up strongly in the last place spot for today. We listened in on the mid race commentary and Daniel seemed much more concerned with “these damn kids and their skateboards and their SnapTok...” than the race.
