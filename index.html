Stage 1 is complete!

Stage one is complete folks! While Chase Marshall took a commanding lead, he was tripped up by some minor inconsistencies in his marble’s surface and finished 6th. Kay, Starrie, and Franklin traded places right up until the finish with Kay taking second (a separation of only .116 seconds I might add!) But our winner for the day is, of course, Bethany Powell. Bethany would like to thank her sponsor, Nina Houghton. Later, she added that she would be celebrating at a nearby Chuck E. Cheeses with some of her friends from space camp. We think this is a bold move the night before stage 2 but support her in her win.

Perhaps the most notable performance of the day was Jeremy Alvarez (deformity: 9.414) finishing in 11th for the stage. In a post-race interview, we asked Jeremy how he pulled off such an incredible feat with his janky marble to which he responded “Hopes and dreams, player. Hopes and dreams.” Remember, there is a prize for first, second, and last place. Stuart Cooper, unfortunately, seemed to run into some mechanical trouble during the final stages of the race and finished in last place but he feels good and is hopeful for tomorrow’s race.
