These stages only exsist as numbers and forces applied to your "marbles." Just use your imagination and get back to what's really important... like rewatching westworld because like seriously... it's been two years.

1

A good, old-fashion incline phase with very few obsticles. This stage seems to be pretty even and doesn't add or take away from your marbles modifiers.

2

A long straight away. Because of its incline, it seems to favor heavier marbles.

3

A curvy long course with many obsticles. Lighter marbles seem to track (marginally) better on this simulation.

4

A long bumpy course. I'll be honest, this is hell on wheels for deformed marbles. Weight doesnt seem to affect performance that much.

5

Because computers a dope, this stage decided to throw physics the hell out. Deformed marbles perform almost 3 times better in this stage because let's face it, it would suck to be an ugly marble.
